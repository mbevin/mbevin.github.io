The latest #1 selling hit iOS puzzle game from CandyCane, now loved by millions of players worldwide, Fling! features thousands of unique puzzles split into 35 levels of increasing difficulty, guaranteeing many hours of puzzling fun!

Each puzzle has a single, unique solution, and is solved by simply flinging (pushing) the furballs, and watching them bump each other off the edge of the screen, until only one is left. View the gameplay video on YouTube to see the game in action!

Key Features:

  • Four unique game modes, to suit all kinds of fast+slow players
  • Local+Global+Facebook high-scores lists for the arcade-mode
  • Automatic saving - always come back to the game right where you left it!
  • Hints + Undos are provided to help you solve the puzzles when stuck
  • Game music (automatically switched off when playing other music on your device)

Now also available on Android and Windows Phone!
